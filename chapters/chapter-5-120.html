You may sometimes read about "hot" and "cold" sources. A hot source is a source where events are being emitted no matter if there are subscribers or not. A cold source is a source where events only start being emitted after the first subscription. A periodic timer is a hot source, while a file to read is a cold source. With a cold source you can get all events, while with a hot source you will only get those emitted after you have subscribed.
