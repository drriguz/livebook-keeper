Using the just factory method is great in examples and tests, but of course in real-world scenarios you will want to adapt a more complex source to produce events to an RxJava observable type. To do that, there is a general-purpose Publisher interface that you can implement to emit items to subscribers using the fromPublisher method (instead of just). There are also adapter methods for JDK futures, iterable objects and also generating items from a JDK callable object.
